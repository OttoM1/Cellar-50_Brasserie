Chardonnay

Oaked: Butter, vanilla. Unoaked: Apple, lemon.

Aromatic. Lime, green apple, petrol (aged), honey.

Young Wines (1–4 Years)

Reds: Brick tones, dried fruit, leather, tobacco.
